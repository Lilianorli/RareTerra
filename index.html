Delivering Low-cost American-made

Rare Earth Metals NOW

RareTerra harnesses the power of biology to make rare earth production clean, circular, and accessible—fueling the technologies that drive our future..

Learn

RareTerra’s mission is to transform rare earth production with biotechnology, delivering low-cost, American-made materials that power clean energy, advanced communications, and consumer technologies. By eliminating toxic chemicals, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and recycling critical minerals, we are building a circular and sustainable supply chain. Guided by innovation, equity, and environmental responsibility, we are training the next generation workforce to lead the future of critical mineral production with biology as our inspiration.
